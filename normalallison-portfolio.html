About

Ordinary life,
radical honesty.

I'm a Kansas City-based mom, editor, and everyday activist — and I've built my online presence deliberately outside the rules of traditional influencing. There are no brand deals here. No sponsored content dressed up as real life. What you see is what's actually happening.

"I called myself an antifluencer because I needed a word for what I actually am — someone who uses this platform to resist, not to sell."

My content lives at the intersection of motherhood and resistance. I'm raising kids in a world that is actively hostile to many of the people we love, and I refuse to let that be invisible. Every post is a small act of showing up — for my community, my city, my conscience.

As an editor and KCMO resident, I bring a writer's eye for accountability to local issues that too often go unnoticed. Kansas City is my beat. The people here are my reason.
